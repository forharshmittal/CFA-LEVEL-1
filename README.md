# CFA Level 1: Financial Modeling & Practice 📈

This repository serves as a centralized portfolio for my financial analysis projects, Excel models, and practice files aligned with the CFA Level 1 curriculum. 

The goal of this project is to translate theoretical financial concepts into practical, data-driven Excel models. Tracking cash flows, breaking down financial records line by line, and valuing equities accurately are at the core of my approach to investment analysis.

## 📂 Repository Structure

The repository is divided into 10 groups, mapping directly to the CFA Level 1 topic areas:

1. **Quantitative Methods:** Time value of money, probability, and statistical concepts.
2. **Economics:** Macro/microeconomic principles and currency exchange.
3. **Financial Statement Analysis (FSA):** Assessing earnings quality, cash flow operating activities, and ratio analysis.
4. **Corporate Issuers:** Cost of capital, leverage, and corporate governance.
5. **Equity Investments:** Equity valuation models and market efficiency.
6. **Fixed Income:** Bond valuation, yield measures, and risk metrics.
7. **Derivatives:** Forward commitments, options, and hedging strategies.
8. **Alternative Investments:** Real estate, private equity, and commodities.
9. **Portfolio Management:** Asset allocation and portfolio risk management.
10. **Ethical and Professional Standards:** Application of the CFA Code of Ethics.
